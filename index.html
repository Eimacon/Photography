Chiptune Music

I guess I tinkered around with the classic 8-bit machines! Hmm.., Looks like I don't have a picture of my beloved GameBoys from 1996. Guess I'll just post my current setup right now. Using a custom catridge and custom softare (Little Sound DJ), one can capture the retro feeling of Video Game Music from the early 90s. While did not grow up playing games, the iconic tunes of the golden age of gaming have left an impression. Programming on a musical tracker is so simple that anyone can do it! I have a few excerts of my repotoire.

I use a alias of DJ Dvorak, a handle given by friends after my adoption of the Dvorak keyboard.

Home Server

I have been getting into woodworking. I also had a bunch of computer parts. I thought it would be cool to make a wooden computer case. The result is not half-bad. It runs on Gentoo for some reason and hosts a variety of web services and personal projects.
